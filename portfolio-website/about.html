    Sienna Roggeveen

    About the Artist

    Sienna Roggeveen is a seventeen-year-old junior at Eastside Preparatory High School. She recently discovered a passion for photography after taking a photography class at school; she enjoys photographing, editing, and playing with different angles. Apart from photography, Sienna finds herself playing piano, rowing, hanging out with her family & friends, singing, working in social justice activism, and lifeguarding in her free time. She is very excited to share her work with you in this portfolio website!

    In the future, Sienna wants to go to college in the west or east coast. She is planning on majoring in biological sciences on a pre-law track. She aspires to learn how to play guitar, speak Korean, songwrite, and continue her photography journey!
