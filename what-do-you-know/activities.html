Social Justice

I am very passionate about social justice, and throughout my high school career I have been learning and educating about worldwide issues. In the Youth Ambassador Program (YAP), we work to inspire other teens to work to improve in the UN's global goals. Each year, the ambassadors plan a Teen Action Fair full of non-profit organizations to inspire other teens. In the compilation of images to the left, you will find images from our March 23, 2024 Teen Action Fair.
In addition to YAP, I'm also working to build a website about healthcare disparities, which is another reason I wanted to take Web 2 this year!

Choir

I joined EPS Chamber Choir in 9th grade after participating in at least 6 trimesters of middle school choir. We rehearse every day at 7:30am and put on EPS performances, carol in the lobby of the nutcracker, and performed at Benaroya Hall in Seattle.

Piano

I have been playing piano for fun since I was just 4 years old. I have always been passionate about music, so making music (both in choir and on the piano) is something that I really enjoy. Although I do not play piano competetively, I love playing for fun.
When I was just 3 years old, I asked my parents to begin piano lessons, and they said I could only start when I turn 4. So, on my fourth birthday (6 months later), I remembered their promise and promptly asked to start piano lessons; I haven't stopped since.
