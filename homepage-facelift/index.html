The Next Step Physical Therapy

Physical Therapy based in Rowing

Welcome to The Next Step Phyiscal Therapy!

We are Seattle’s premier Physical Therapy clinic working with patients of all ages and backgrounds. Our owner and sole practitioner is Karen Calara who has over 27 years of experience in the Seattle area. Combining specialties in sports biomechanics, manual therapy, functional exercise, and strength training and conditioning, Karen creates comprehensive programs tailored to the individual. My holistic approach makes the most impact by treating patients one on one, with hour long appointments. Our patients receive custom solutions that best fit their goals, needs and lifestyle. They love the fact I use an integrated approach and feel the changes are more lasting.
