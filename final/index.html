Who are we?

Queen Anne Dispatch is your neighborhood one stop shopping and shipping location. Often referred to as “your neighborhood Concierge”, you would be surprised at the services we offer. If you need something, or need to have something done, stop on by. Chances are, if we don’t, we can sure help you find someone who does. Queen Anne Dispatch’s goal is to be able to offer more to its customers and constantly evolve to better serve our community.
